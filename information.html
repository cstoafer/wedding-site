Accommodations

There are a number of hotels within walking distance of the venue. We have setup room blocks at the following places, please let them know that you are attending the Brandes-Stoafer wedding when you make your reservation.

The Soundings Seaside Resort

Address: 79 Chase Avenue, Dennis Port, MA 02639
Phone for reservations: (888) 221-1160, let them know you are attending the Brandes-Stoafer wedding
Website: www.bluegreenvacations.com/resorts/ma/soundings-seaside-resort

The Soundings is a beachside resort across the street from the wedding venue that has both 1 bedroom and 2 bedroom condo units. The bride and groom will be staying here for the wedding weekend.




The Gaslight

Address: 82 Chase Ave, Dennis Port, MA 02639
Phone: (508) 398-8831, let them know you are attending the Brandes-Stoafer wedding
Website: www.gaslightmotel.com

The Gaslight Motel is a charming family owned and operated motel located next door to the wedding venue.
